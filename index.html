Key Insights

Top Brands: The leading cosmetics brands are Clinique and Shiseido, with moisturizers dominating sales across the market.

Skin Compatibility: Most products, particularly from Clinique, are not optimally suited for sensitive, dry, and normal skin types, indicating a gap in the market for specialized formulations.

Premium Positioning: La Mer maintains its position as the luxury leader with the highest-priced items in the category.

Quality Ratings: Top brands and product categories consistently maintain high customer satisfaction ratings, averaging between 4.0 to 4.5 stars.
